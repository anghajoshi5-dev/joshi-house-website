Joshi House Pictures is an independent film production house based in Mumbai, producing films grounded in realism, intimacy, and distinct South Asian voices.

About Joshi House Pictures

Founded to tell intimate, character-driven stories from South Asia, Joshi House Pictures partners with writers and directors to develop original voices. We focus on films that travel — stories with a universal soul that find audiences globally.
